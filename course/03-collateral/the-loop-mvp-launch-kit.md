## PART 3 — Positioning & Growth Ladder

### The positioning statement (memorize this)
**For people who use AI but are drowning in it, The Loop is the production system that makes AI build WITH you — so nothing gets lost and you never start over.** Unlike prompt-tip gurus and AI-expert courses, it's taught by a non-expert who built the course with the system itself — receipts included.

### The three positioning rules
1. **Never sell AI expertise. Sell rescue.** Your competitors say "I'm the expert." You say "I was drowning, here's the rope." That's un-copyable — they'd have to give up their guru status to match it.
2. **Own one word: Loop.** Every product, post, and lesson uses the vocabulary — Session Loop, Review Loop, Launch Loop. When people say "close the loop" about their AI work, you've won the category.
3. **Proof over promises.** Show the receipts: version history, the change log, before/after of your own chaos. Screenshots of the actual "Help me!" moment are worth more than any testimonial.

### The value ladder (each rung sells the next)
- **FREE — The Session Close template + Read/Skim/Skip card** (one page). Lead magnet: "the 5-minute routine that ends starting-over." Collects emails; delivers a win in one day.
- **$12.99 — Quickstart Edition (written course)** (live now). The system in writing. Founding students = your future testimonials and case studies.
- **$97 — Full Video Edition + Upgraded Launch playbook.** Filmed lessons (your videographers), the complete Module 0-5 curriculum, including the Launch Loop (promoter playbook). Presale at $47 (Part 4). Founding students get first dibs.
- **$997+ — The Loop Immersive: In-Person in Hawaii.** Small-group intensive (cap ~10-15): build your blueprint and run your first full Loop live, with direct help, in a destination setting no online course can match. Unscalable-at-the-top by design (per S-001 principle). Rules: sell only AFTER the video edition ships and you have student results to showcase; $997 is the seed price for cohort #1 — a travel-worthy destination workshop can honestly command $1,500-2,500 once proven, so raise it after the first sells out. Video-edition students get first invitations and alumni pricing.
- **Later — Done-with-you / B2B.** Teams pay 10x for the same system installed. Park until the ladder below is running.

### The growth engine (three loops, of course)
1. **The Buyer Loop:** every buyer gets asked "What are you trying to build?" Their answers → your content, your FAQ, your video-edition script. Their results → case studies that sell the next rung.
2. **The Content Loop:** each course lesson = 3-5 short posts (the origin story, Read/Skim/Skip, the four ways AI systems rot, you-can't-break-it). You already own the content; recontextualize it — practice what you teach.
3. **The Promoter Loop (from S-001):** when the $97 edition launches, run the two-tier promoter offer from Module 5 — free upgrades for people who bring 3+ friends, something unscalable (a live teardown of their setup) for the top 10. Your own launch becomes the Module 5 case study.

### Upsell timing rules
- Pitch the $97 edition inside the Quickstart only ONCE (the "What's Next" page) — over-pitching a $12.99 product kills trust.
- Email founding students value 4 times for every 1 ask.
- Raise the Quickstart to $19-29 after the founding window (30 days or 100 students) — scarcity that's real, because you announced it.
