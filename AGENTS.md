We are in a sales sprint, not a build phase. The bottleneck right now is **not**
infrastructure — it is that no real local business owner has seen the offer and said yes.

Automation amplifies a working motion; it does not create one. So we are NOT building the
fully-automated machine yet. We are getting the offer in front of warm people manually,
learning what converts, and automating only the steps that prove themselves on live leads.

The recurring failure mode for this account is **building instead of shipping.** This file
exists to break that loop.
