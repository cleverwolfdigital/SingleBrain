Getting Started

How to Use Single Brain

Your command center for every business, project, and decision. Here's the two-minute tour.

1

Signing in

Go to brain.cleverwolfdigital.com, enter your @cleverwolfdigital.com email, and click the magic link we email you. First time only: scan the QR with an authenticator app (Google or Microsoft Authenticator), then enter the 6-digit code. After that, just the code each login.

2

Navigating

Use the left sidebar. Overview is today's brief. Businesses lists all 29, grouped Tier 1–4 by owner + status. Projects shows active work and the next action. Kimchee #88 holds the restaurant launch tasks. Daily Rotation is the weekly cadence. Staff, Blockers, Recommendations cover people, risks, and ranked next moves.

3

Quick Add a task

Click Quick add (top-right, or on the Projects page). Enter the task, pick a business, category, priority, and due date. You can link dependencies to other tasks. Save — it lands in the Action Queue.

4

Daily Journal

Open Daily Journal each morning to set your one focus and top 3 priorities, and at end of day to log wins, decisions, and tomorrow's first action.

5

Brain Chat

Brain Chat is your conversational view — ask about priorities, projects, and decisions across the whole portfolio.

6

Security & logout

Access is limited to @cleverwolfdigital.com accounts with magic link + authenticator (2FA). Click the log-out icon (top-right) when you're done — especially on shared devices.
